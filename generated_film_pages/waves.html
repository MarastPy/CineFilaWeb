Logline

The film explores waves in nature and life through dramatic moments, human fragments, and flowing movements in sea, grass, and branches.

Synopsis

This short author film is being created using sand animation, and explores the theme of waves in various forms and contexts. A collage of situations from non-specific places around the globe, and the rhythmical motion and sound of waves occuring naturally in the world around us.
