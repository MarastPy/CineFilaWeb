Logline

A giant beetroot goes on a rampage in this wild Slavic kaiju tale mixing live action, stop-motion, and 2D animation.

Synopsis

Inspired by the traditional Slavic folktale “The Enormous Turnip,” a beetroot grows so huge that no one can pull it from the ground. When a local crane operator is hired to do the job, the giant vegetable turns violent and destructive. As chaos unfolds, an army is mobilized to stop it — but maybe there’s another way to appease this monstrous force.

Director's Note

Beetroot is a collaboration between two directors from FAMU and UMPRUM, blending distinct authorial styles. Inspired by the classic Czech tale The Big Beetroot, we reimagine the beet as an uncontrollable force, merging fairy tale with the Japanese Kaiju genre. As the beet grows, so do the problems—nature's power takes monstrous form.
