Logline

On New Year's Eve 1944, two Sudeten men—one a widower, the other an SS officer—confront a shared past in a snowbound cottage cut off from the world.

Synopsis

On New Year’s Eve 1944, widower Petr Bernat and SS officer Konrad Neumann meet in a remote cottage during a snowstorm. United only by their Sudeten origins, their conversation shifts from trivial topics to painful truths, leading to a fateful night for both men.
