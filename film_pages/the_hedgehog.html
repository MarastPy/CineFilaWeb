Logline

Can hedgehog swim?

Synopsis

Slightly moralising pun, filmed with pleasant exaggeration and a sense of animation shorthand. A child's imagination knows no bounds, and anyone who gets too carried away, whether drawing or just looking at a picture, can end up getting really wet. A hedgehog like that doesn't seem to be a very good swimmer, but he's all the more powerful.
